Tips for improvement

Here are some suggestions for how to improve your health in different areas. Click on category to view tips.

    • Limit caffeine after 3pm
      Avoid screens 1 hour before bedtime
      Set your alarm clock for the same time everyday

    • Start slowly and gradually build up the duration of the exercises
      Find an exercise buddy or personal coach to stay accountable
      Make it enjoyable - listen to your favourite music, track your progress, practice creative sports like danc

    • Get a good water bottle that you can take everywhere with you
      Set alarms to remind you in case you forget
      Add fruit or veg to your water, such as lemons, bluberries or cucmber to make it more tasy.

    • Swap out processed sweet foods for fruit
      Make sure you eat enough healthy fats and protein to keep feeling satiated
      Try cutting out sugar completely for a week and notice how your taste changes

    • Turn off your devices and if you live with others tell them you want to be uninterrupted for 10 minutes
      Choose a dedicated space that you can go to every time you want some quiet time - it maybe favourite armchair, bench park or spot in your garden
      Try doing breathing mindfulness exercises or journalling prompts

    • Create frequent reminders to check your posture while using a screen, this will make you more likely to be aware of your body and take breaks for movement
      Try to use analog items to complete tasks, such as physical notebook, calculator and alarm clock
      Create rules for no screens zones and times, such as no screens in the bedroom or while eating or even regularly take the whole day without screens

    • Spend quality time together and share experiences
      Communicate your needs and expectations clearly and learn active listening skills
      Set personal boundaries, which means what YOU will do in a certain situation (and the other person does nothing)

    • Identify what would bring your community together, e.g. a hobby, activity that you do together
      Foster strong relationships with people in the community by spending regular quality time together
      Communicate to establish joint culture of the group - what behaviours are problematic and how to support each other

    • Identify your values and passions and explore your strength and interests
      Find ways to delegate or automate tasks that drain your energy
      Define your personal mission statement

    • Take time to do things that you enjoy and try new things to find what gives you life
      Set goals for growth and practice self-awareness
      Give back to make a difference by volunteering, donating money and practicing kindness
